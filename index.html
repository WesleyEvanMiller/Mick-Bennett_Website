Missing You In Belmar, NJ
M

issing You in Belmar, NJ is the first novel of a trilogy which takes place in the summer of 1987. The back cover blurb concludes with a long sentence which begins: In Jimmy’s story of redemption… Merriam-Webster defines redemption as follows: the act of making something better or more acceptable. The novel is about a man trying to belong again—to his family, community, and by extension, his country. Hemingway stated, “There is nothing noble in being superior to your fellow man; true nobility is being superior to your former self.” Missing You is a quest to find such nobility. I invite you to join Jimmy on his journey.
